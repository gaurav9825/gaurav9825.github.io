30 July 2018

It all started in the month of March when i applied for frontend intern in the company which was started by our alumni's.I dropped a mail to hr expressing my interest to join the company. I got reply after three days with a link to the test to be submitted within 48 hours.I somehow completed the task and submiited it. Then i went through a series of interviews and Finally got selected.It was 10 weeks internship.

I was quite excited for the internship and the same time nervous too as it was my first internship and i had no clue how things actually work in the corporate world.

Never believed that I would get the opportunity to intern at a startup like HackerEarth — just after the 2nd year of college and yet it happened! That moment gave me a sense of achievement and joy — something that makes me believe in dreaming big, after all, it does not cost one to dream.

Journey Begins

I left for bangalore on 6th of May, my intern period was from 7th May 18 to 17 Jul 18. It was my first time travelling to an unknown city and that too alone, i was quite nervous but i knew that these ten weeks of my life would be the best time in my life.In the office i was welcomed by my buddy Akansha Valecha, who was the chief frontend enginner of the sprint team (my team).She was quite nice to me and always helped me in every possible manner.

Hackerearth has a culture of pairing each new intern with a buddy who help you with everything ranging from finding a good pg to helping you with your work.

Work Culture

Hackerearth has a very flexible work culture. There is no specified time limit which you have to spend in the office,as long as your work is complete. Everyone is treated equally in the company, there is no cabin culture, everone is accessible everytime and you can even walk to the CTO anytime with your problem and he will be happy to help.

Don't be an asshole ~ Hackerearth

My work involved around HackerEarth's new product called as Sprint. The old product has a lot of design flaws and and the website was not responsive. I was assigned to work on the entire redesign of the product and it took me almost a month to complete the task and the result was completely resonsive for desktop as well all other devices.

Thank You

HackerEarth is a great place to work at, learn, and grow. I was fortunate to spend 3 months working there and contributing to the product. I will always remember this time. Thanks for the chance and for believing in me.
